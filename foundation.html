Foundation

A Collaborative, Community Driven Organization

Crypti was founded by a five-member team who set out to create a community driven evolution of the crypto currency concept. During the pre-launch phase and early development, the core founders created the concepts that would propel Crypti to what, at the time of launch, was one of the most successful crowd-funded pre-sales of any crypto currency in history.

One of the core tenants that lead to such a successful launch was the inclusion of the community in early decisions and the constant open communication and discourse with anyone willing to get involved. From day one, the Crypti team has focused on ensuring that the communities voice is heard and that Crypti isn’t just built by a small team, but rather by the community as a whole.

With this goal in mind, the founding members formed The Crypti Foundation. All post-launch development and funds raised in the pre-sale period are managed by the Crypti Foundation. The Foundation is an organization comprised of both the original five founding members of the Crypti development team, as well as influential community members who were hand selected by the community to represent their interests and be the guiding voice of the community on the Crypti Foundation Board of Directors.

Together, the Foundation members manage all post-launch development as well as all funds raised in the pre-sale period. The Foundation concept truly epitomizes the spirit with which Crypti was created and provides for a collaborative and open environment. If you would like to get more involved in the Foundation, join the conversation over at our official forums or the official Crypti BTT Thread and see how you can help!
