Perfect for Developers

Build your own custom application or crypto-currency and harness the power of the Crypti network through our Custom Block Chain consensus and verification system.

Powered by DPOS

Crypti uses a custom delegated proof of stake system built to reward trusted users. We have taken the work pioneered by the Bitshares team and added our own flavor to the mix. Our DPOS is one of the most efficient, secure, and user-powered systems in the Crypto space.

Community Driven Development

Crypti is developed by a team that is split 50/50 between the original development team and real community members. We don’t have global science awards, but we are dedicated, hard-working, and we listen. We have taken feedback from the community since our pre-alpha to help shape the future of Crypti and we wouldn't have it any other way! Learn more
