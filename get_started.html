Get Started

Step 1 - Create an account at our web wallet

To get started using Crypti, you first need to create an account using our Web Wallet.

All Crypti accounts use what is called a Brain Wallet. To keep it simple, a brain wallet associates your account with the secret pass phrase used to initially create that account. Due to this fact, it makes it essential that you keep track of your passphrase at all times and keep it as secure as possible.

As an added precaution, Crypti has implemented the option to pay a small fee and implement a second passphrase. This second passphrase is highly recommended and if implemented, is required to send money from your account. In the case that your first passphrase is compromised, no hacker would be able to remove funds from your account without also knowing the second passphrase as well.

Step 2 - Get some Crypti

There are several ways to go about getting your first few XCR. You can start by getting free XCR at our Crypti Faucet. The amount varies and it well help get you started.

You can also head over to our Official Bounties Board or our Community Bounties Board and see if there is something you can contribute to the community in order to earn some XCR.

The easiest way to get XCR is to head over to one of the many exchanges that list Crypti and buy some on the open market. You can currently trade Crypti on the following exchanges:

  • BTER
  • Cryptsy
  • Poloniex
